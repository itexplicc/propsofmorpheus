# Nautical Naturals Brand Website

A complete multi-page responsive brand website.

## Pages
Home, Products, Daily Diet, Ingredients, Nutrition, Feeding Guide, Our Story,
Sustainability, Retail & Trade, FAQ, Contact, Private Review, Privacy, Terms and 404.

## Forms
- Contact and trade forms write privately to `nautical_naturals_inquiries`.
- George's review form writes privately to `nautical_naturals_feedback`.
- Public website users have insert-only access; no public read policy is used.
