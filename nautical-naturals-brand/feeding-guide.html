Feeding support

A calmer way to change the bowl.

Every dog is different. Start gradually, use the final pack guidance, watch body condition and adjust with professional support when needed.

Move slowly over seven days.

A gradual transition gives the digestive system time to adapt.

Consistency matters.

01

Measure meals

Use the feeding range on the final commercial pack as your starting point.

02

Keep water available

Fresh water should be available throughout the day.

03

Limit uncounted extras

Treats and table food affect the total daily intake.

Watch the dog, not only the bowl.

Body condition

Look for steady condition rather than rapid gain or loss.

Digestion

Monitor stool quality, comfort and tolerance during transition.

Energy

Changes in activity, age or environment may change feeding needs.

Professional advice

Speak with a veterinarian for medical conditions, pregnancy, growth concerns or unusual changes.

Bowl planner

Choose a sensible starting approach.

This planner does not replace the feeding table on the final pack. It helps you decide where inside that range to begin.
