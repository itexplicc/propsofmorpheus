Questions, answered

Clear information belongs beside clear nutrition.

Explore common questions about the brand, Daily Diet, feeding and commercial availability.

Nautical Naturals is a fish-first dog nutrition brand built around real fish, marine-rich nourishment and a clear sea-and-soil ingredient philosophy.

Daily Diet is the core all-life-stages product direction at the centre of the Nautical Naturals range.

Individual suitability depends on the final commercial formula, the dog’s age, health, activity, allergies and professional advice. Always review the final pack and consult a veterinarian for specific medical needs.

Transition gradually over approximately seven days, moving from mostly current food to fully Nautical Naturals only if the dog is adapting well.

The validated commercial pack will be the definitive source for the final ingredient declaration, guaranteed analysis, calorie information and feeding table.

The brand direction is built around avoiding artificial colours, flavours and preservatives. Final statements must match the validated commercial formula.

It means fish and marine ingredients form the defining nutritional and brand foundation rather than appearing as a minor background component.
