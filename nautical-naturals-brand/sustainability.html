Responsibility

The sea inspires the brand. Respect must guide the business.

Sustainability is not a decorative seal. It is a system of sourcing questions, supplier evidence, packaging choices and continuous improvement.

Our responsibility framework

Evidence before claims.

01

Seafood sourcing

Supplier origin, species, traceability and sourcing practices must be reviewed before claims are made.

02

Ingredient efficiency

Use valuable marine resources thoughtfully and seek credible use of suitable co-products where appropriate.

03

Packaging discipline

Protect food quality first, then reduce unnecessary material and improve recyclability as formats allow.

04

Clear communication

Do not use broad environmental language without documentation that supports it.

05

Supplier standards

Commercial relationships should include quality, compliance and responsible-practice expectations.

06

Continuous improvement

Responsible sourcing is an operating discipline, not a one-time launch statement.

Our public standard

We will say only what we can support.

Any future sustainability statement on a pack, product page or sales document should be tied to current supplier evidence and the exact commercial recipe.
