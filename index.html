Commercial logic

The bigger the load, the better the economics

Small quantities are possiblePay for the relevant mold and we can manufacture 100, 200, 500, or another practical quantity.
Mix products on one palletClassic, Roadie, and Stealth may be combined to fill the same pallet, subject to packing efficiency.
Below one pallet costs more per unitShipping is still approximately $750 whether the pallet carries 100, 800, or the full 1,100 pieces.
Container scale improves costAt larger container volumes, indicative production cost is approximately $3.00–$3.75 per unit before mold and shipping.
20-pallet shipping benchmarkApproximately $11,000 shipping for a 20-pallet container, versus $15,000 at the standard $750-per-pallet basis.
Flexible mold ownershipThe buyer may purchase and own the mold, or a factory-funded mold arrangement can be discussed for larger committed volumes.
