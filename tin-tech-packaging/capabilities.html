The capability stack

Eight connected disciplines under one project.

Tin Tech can manufacture, source, assemble and coordinate the complete product presentation where the program requires it.

01 · DEFINE

Product brief & feasibility

Translate the commercial idea into a reviewable manufacturing brief.

  • Intended use and target market
  • Dimensions, function and performance
  • Target volume, timing and budget
  • Make-versus-source assessment
02 · ENGINEER

Design & DFM

Develop the geometry and production logic before committing to tooling.

  • Drawings and dimensional review
  • Wall thickness, draft and assembly
  • Closure feel and fit testing
  • Sample and approval pathway
03 · TOOL

Mold making & ownership

Structure the correct tooling route and document ownership terms.

  • Existing platform or modified tooling
  • Dedicated client-owned molds
  • Maintenance and storage terms
  • Exclusivity and change control
04 · SOURCE

Materials & components

Match the resin and sourced components to the product and presentation.

  • Commodity and engineering resins
  • Metal lids, closures and inserts
  • Labels, cartons and retail packaging
  • Supplier coordination and validation
05 · PRODUCE

Injection molding & repeat runs

Move approved products into controlled commercial production.

  • Production planning and capacity
  • Color and finish programs
  • In-process quality checkpoints
  • Repeat records and run control
06 · BRAND

Customization & private label

Build the product around the brand, not just the basic function.

  • Molded logos and detail
  • Printing and label systems
  • Color matching and line extensions
  • Premium and practical finish routes
07 · PACK

Assembly & pack-out

Coordinate the components and presentation before the shipment leaves.

  • Multi-part assembly
  • Bulk, retail and private-label packing
  • Carton planning and export protection
  • Barcode and insert coordination
08 · DELIVER

Freight & onward coordination

Plan the route from Sri Lanka to warehouse or delivery destination.

  • Export documentation support
  • Freight and Incoterm review
  • New York warehouse facilitation
  • Onward delivery planning
09 · SCALE

Dedicated production programs

Move beyond project-by-project supply when the volume supports it.

  • Reserved capacity and repeat schedules
  • Dedicated lines and QC plans
  • Operating partnerships
  • Sri Lanka factory pathway

Important project note

Certification is product-specific.

Food-contact, child-resistant, regulatory or other certification claims are made only where the exact material, product design, intended use, test method and supporting records apply.

Tin Tech distinguishes ordinary manufacturing documentation from product-specific testing and certification requirements before a claim is made.
