Common questions

What buyers usually ask first.

Do I need a finished CAD drawing?

No. A physical sample, images, measurements or a clear explanation can begin the review. Formal technical information is developed before tooling approval.

Can Tin Tech support lower-volume launches?

Some products can begin with flexible commercial runs, while others require higher quantities because of tooling, resin, decoration or packaging economics.

Who owns the mold?

Mold ownership, storage, maintenance and exclusivity are written into each proposal before payment. Dedicated client-owned tooling can be structured where appropriate.

Can delivery be coordinated to my destination?

Yes. Export freight, New York warehousing and onward delivery options can be coordinated. Scope and cost depend on shipment size, destination and fulfillment needs.

Are products automatically certified for every use?

No. Requirements depend on the product, material, claims and intended use. Testing and certification are defined project by project.
