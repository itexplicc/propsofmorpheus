About Tin Tech

A manufacturing company built around long-term product programs.

Tin Tech Packaging combines Sri Lankan production capability with practical commercial coordination, helping brands move from product concept to repeatable supply.

What makes the model differentDevelopment, tooling, production, branding, packing and delivery planning are handled as one connected program—not a chain of disconnected suppliers.
The company

Made in Sri Lanka. Managed for dependable export supply.

Tin Tech works with businesses that need more than a factory quotation. The aim is to understand the product, define the right manufacturing route, maintain repeat quality and stay accountable as the program grows.
