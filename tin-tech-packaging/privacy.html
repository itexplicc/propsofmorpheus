How website information is handled.

Last updated: July 2026

Project inquiries

When you submit the project form, Tin Tech stores the information you provide so the team can review and respond to the manufacturing request. This may include your name, company, email, phone, product details, target quantity and delivery destination.

How the information is used

The information is used to assess the project, prepare questions or proposals, coordinate follow-up and maintain a record of commercial communication. It is not sold as marketing data.

Portfolio administration

The admin portal is restricted by a shared password and is intended for authorized Tin Tech personnel. Product images and catalog data are stored in the company backend.

Data retention and requests

Project information may be retained while the opportunity or client relationship remains relevant. To request correction or deletion, email hockeyundergroundusa@gmail.com.
